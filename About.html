About DingGuo Game

  • We are a group of **Scratch enthusiasts**, and we thank Scratch for providing great programming tools for children. ScratchJr is an introductory tool for children aged 5-7 to learn Scratch, officially provided by Scratch. Compared with Scratch, ScratchJr is more suitable for beginners.
  • We are the initiator of the open-source web version of ScratchJr. Find our project here: https://github.com/wangzongjun/ScratchJr
